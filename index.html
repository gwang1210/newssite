Marco Rubio Drops Out of the Race

Florida Senator and hopeful GOP nominee Marco Rubio ends his 2016 presidential campaign today. The trail has been a messy one, with unexpected power player Trump and rival Cruz playing against him. On Tuesday night, Rubio did not manage to win Florida; the senators home state went to Trump with 49 percent of the vote and Rubio coming in a distant second. Hours later Rubio suspended his campaign only to stall the inevitable.

School Gets Unexpected Visitor

Students at Geneva Classical Academy in Lakeland received an unexpected guest today. Their visiter: a 9ft 11in gator. The gator wandered onto school grounds early Thursday morning. The school called a trapper, but allowed teachers and students to spectate and take videos.

Lakeland Memorial Unveiled

The Global War on Terrorism Freedom Memorial was unveiled in Veterans Memorial Park on Saturday morning. The memorial is a sculpture of a "soldier's cross," and honors those who have made the ultimate sacrifice to protect their country.
